# Advocado Toast with poached eggs

## Servings: 2

## Time to prepare: 30 min

## Ingredients: 
Egg*2
Avocado*1
Toast*2
Salt & Pepper

## Equipment needed:
Frying Pan
Oven
Pot

## Steps for preparation:
1. Cut the Avacado
2. poached the egg
3. Toast the bread
4. Assemble them and season with salt and pepper
